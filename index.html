A Ketchikan Tradition Every year at Schoenbar Middle School students participate in a school wide outdoor education unit: beginning survival techniques. The objective of this unit is for students to gain confidence and develop the skills necessary to handle outdoor environments and to enjoy outdoor activities in Southeast Alaska.
